My Interests

Guitar

I enjoy playing guitar across multiple genres
including rock, blues, and jazz.
I also write and arrange music,
experimenting with both acoustic and electric guitars.


Golf

Golf is one of my main sports hobbies.
I practice regularly to improve my swing
and aim to lower my handicap below 20.
I also enjoy the strategic and mental aspects of the game.


Sports

Baseball and football are two sports I follow
and play casually.
I enjoy teamwork and competition,
and often analyze games to improve strategy understanding.


Video Games

Video gaming is a creative outlet
and a way to study game mechanics, design, and storytelling.
I enjoy both single-player adventures
and competitive multiplayer games.


Skiing

I enjoy skiing both recreationally
and as a way to challenge myself physically.
Exploring different trails and improving technique
are key parts of this interest.


Climbing

Rock climbing and indoor climbing gyms
are a way for me to stay fit and focused.
I enjoy problem-solving climbing routes
and pushing my physical limits safely.
